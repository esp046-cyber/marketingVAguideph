üì± Social Media Management

‚ñº
  • Daily Posting Schedule Post 3-5 times daily across platforms. Best times: 9AM, 12PM, 6PM PHT. Use scheduling tools like Buffer or Hootsuite.
  • Content Calendar Planning Plan 2 weeks ahead. Mix: 40% promotional, 30% educational, 30% engagement. Track holidays and trends.
  • Community Engagement Respond to comments within 2 hours. Like, reply, and engage with followers. Monitor brand mentions daily.
  • Hashtag Strategy Use 10-15 hashtags per post. Mix: 5 trending, 5 niche, 5 branded. Research competitors' hashtags.
  • Stories & Reels Post 2-3 stories daily. Create 1-2 Reels weekly. Use trending audio and effects. 15-30 second duration.
üí° Pro Tip: Save high-performing posts as templates. Analyze which content types get most engagement and replicate success.

‚úçÔ∏è Content Creation

‚ñº
  • Graphics Design Use Canva for social posts (1080x1080 for Instagram, 1200x628 for Facebook). Maintain brand colors and fonts.
  • Video Editing Create short-form videos using CapCut or InShot. Add captions, transitions, and brand watermark.
  • Copywriting Write compelling captions (150-200 characters). Use emojis, CTAs, and questions to boost engagement.
  • Blog Content Write 800-1200 word SEO-optimized articles. Include keywords, meta descriptions, and internal links.
  • User-Generated Content Curate and repost customer content (with permission). Tag original creators and use branded hashtags.
üí° Pro Tip: Batch create content on Mondays. Design 10-15 posts at once for efficiency. Store in organized folders.

üìß Email Marketing

‚ñº
  • Campaign Setup Create campaigns in Mailchimp/Klaviyo. Segment lists by behavior, location, and preferences.
  • Email Design Mobile-first templates. Clear CTA buttons. 40% text, 60% visuals. Test on multiple devices.
  • Subject Lines Keep under 50 characters. Use personalization and urgency. A/B test different versions.
  • Automation Flows Set up welcome series, abandoned cart, and re-engagement emails. Trigger based on user actions.
  • List Management Clean lists monthly. Remove inactive subscribers. Comply with GDPR and data privacy laws.
üí° Pro Tip: Best send times: Tuesday-Thursday, 10AM or 2PM. Avoid Mondays and weekends for B2B emails.

üìä Analytics & Reporting

‚ñº
  • Social Media Metrics Track: Reach, Engagement Rate, Follower Growth, Click-through Rate. Use native insights and analytics tools.
  • Website Analytics Monitor Google Analytics: Traffic sources, bounce rate, conversion rate, top pages. Set up goals.
  • Email Performance Key metrics: Open rate (20-25%), Click rate (2-5%), Unsubscribe rate (<0.5%). Identify top-performing emails.
  • Weekly Reports Create visual reports with charts. Highlight wins, challenges, and recommendations. Compare week-over-week.
  • Competitor Analysis Monitor competitors' content, engagement, and campaigns. Identify gaps and opportunities.
üí° Pro Tip: Create dashboard templates for quick weekly reporting. Focus on actionable insights, not just numbers.

üìû Client Management & Communication

‚ñº
  • Client Onboarding Checklist Collect: Brand guidelines, login credentials, content calendar, target audience info. Set expectations on deliverables and timelines.
  • Daily/Weekly Reporting Send EOD (End of Day) updates via email/Slack. Weekly summary every Friday with tasks completed, metrics, and next week's plan.
  • Running Client Meetings Prepare agenda 24hrs before. Take notes during call. Send meeting minutes within 2 hours. Action items with deadlines clearly stated.
  • Handling Difficult Clients Stay professional and calm. Document all communications. Set clear boundaries. Know when to escalate or walk away.
  • Invoice & Payment Tracking Use PayPal, Wise, or Payoneer. Invoice on same date monthly. Follow up on late payments after 7 days. Keep payment records organized.
  • Contract Essentials Include: Scope of work, deliverables, payment terms, revision policy, termination clause. Use digital signatures (DocuSign, HelloSign).
üí° Pro Tip: Create template responses for common client questions. Save 30+ minutes daily with pre-written professional replies.

üéØ Paid Advertising (FB & Google Ads)

‚ñº
  • Facebook Ads Setup Create Business Manager account. Install Meta Pixel on website. Set up Ads Manager. Start with conversion objective campaigns.
  • Audience Targeting Core audiences: Demographics, interests, behaviors. Custom audiences: Website visitors, email lists. Lookalike audiences: 1-3% similarity.
  • Budget Management Start with ‚Ç±500-1000/day test budget. Use CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization). Monitor Cost Per Result. Scale winning ad sets by 20% every 3 days.
  • Ad Creative Best Practices Use 1080x1080 for feed, 1080x1920 for stories. Video ads: 15-30 seconds. Hook in first 3 seconds. Clear CTA button.
  • A/B Testing Test one variable at a time: Image vs video, headline variations, audience types. Run for 3-7 days minimum. 95% confidence level.
  • Google Ads Campaigns Search ads: Keyword research with Google Keyword Planner. Display ads: Banner sizes 300x250, 728x90. Shopping ads: Product feed optimization.
  • Ad Performance Metrics Track: CTR (1-2% good), CPC, CPM, ROAS (3:1 minimum), Conversion rate. Use UTM parameters for tracking.
üí° Pro Tip: Save high-performing ads to "Creative Library". Reuse winning formulas with fresh visuals. Check ads daily at 10AM for optimal performance.

üõçÔ∏è E-commerce & Shopify Management

‚ñº
  • Product Listing Optimization Write SEO titles (60 chars max). Use high-quality images (2000x2000px). Detailed descriptions with bullet points. Include size charts and FAQs.
  • Order Processing Check orders 3x daily (9AM, 1PM, 5PM). Process within 24 hours. Send tracking numbers immediately. Update order status in Shopify.
  • Customer Service Respond to inquiries within 2 hours. Use canned responses for FAQs. Handle returns/refunds professionally. Resolve issues before escalation.
  • Inventory Management Update stock levels daily. Set low-stock alerts (10 units). Coordinate with suppliers weekly. Use inventory apps (Stocky, TradeGecko).
  • Essential Shopify Apps Oberlo (dropshipping), Klaviyo (email), Yotpo (reviews), Privy (popups), ReConvert (upsells), Lucky Orange (heatmaps).
  • Discounts & Promotions Create discount codes strategically. BOGO offers, free shipping thresholds. Flash sales (24-48 hours). Abandoned cart discounts (10-15%).
  • Store Analytics Monitor: Conversion rate (2-3% average), AOV (Average Order Value), Cart abandonment rate. Weekly sales reports with trends.
üí° Pro Tip: Optimize product pages for mobile first (70% of e-commerce traffic). Fast loading speed = higher conversions. Compress all images.

üé• Video Marketing & YouTube

‚ñº
  • YouTube Channel Optimization Channel name with keywords. Compelling banner (2560x1440). Complete "About" section. Add social links. Create playlists by topic.
  • Video SEO Keyword-rich titles (60 chars). Detailed descriptions (200+ words). Tags: 10-15 relevant keywords. Custom thumbnails (1280x720, faces with emotion).
  • Thumbnail Creation Use Canva YouTube template. Bold text (3-5 words max). High contrast colors. Show faces with expressions. Consistent brand style.
  • Video Content Calendar Upload 2-3x weekly minimum. Best days: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday. Post between 2PM-4PM EST. Plan 1 month ahead.
  • YouTube Analytics Track: Watch time, CTR (4-10% good), Average view duration (50%+), Subscriber growth. Check "Traffic sources" for optimization.
  • Short-Form Video Strategy YouTube Shorts: 15-60 seconds vertical. Hook in first 3 seconds. Use trending sounds. Post 1-2 Shorts daily for growth.
  • Long-Form vs Short-Form Long-form (8-15 min): Deep tutorials, storytelling. Short-form (<60s): Quick tips, entertainment. Mix both for algorithm boost.
  • Video Editing Workflow Use CapCut or DaVinci Resolve. Add: Intro (5s), B-roll, captions, transitions, outro with CTA. Export in 1080p minimum.
üí° Pro Tip: First 48 hours are critical. Share new videos across all social platforms. Reply to every comment in first 24 hours to boost engagement.

üí∞ Lead Generation & Sales Funnels

‚ñº
  • Landing Page Creation Use Unbounce, Leadpages, or ClickFunnels. Clear headline with value proposition. Single CTA (no distractions). Mobile-optimized design.
  • Lead Magnet Ideas Free ebooks, checklists, templates, webinars, free trials, discount codes. Must solve specific problem. Deliver via email instantly.
  • Opt-in Form Optimization Keep fields minimal (name + email only). Clear benefit statement. Privacy assurance. A/B test button colors and copy.
  • CRM Setup & Management Use HubSpot, Zoho, or Pipedrive. Tag leads by source, interest, behavior. Create custom fields. Set up automation workflows.
  • Lead Nurture Sequences Welcome email (immediate). Educational content (Days 2, 4, 7). Case studies (Day 10). Sales offer (Day 14). Re-engagement (Day 30).
  • Sales Funnel Stages Awareness ‚Üí Interest ‚Üí Decision ‚Üí Action. Create content for each stage. Track conversion rates between stages.
  • Conversion Rate Optimization Test: Headlines, CTA buttons, form length, page layout. Use heatmaps (Hotjar, Crazy Egg). Aim for 2-5% conversion rate.
  • Lead Scoring System Assign points: Email opens (+5), link clicks (+10), page visits (+15), form fills (+50). Prioritize hot leads (70+ points).
üí° Pro Tip: Follow up with leads within 5 minutes of opt-in. Speed increases conversion by 400%. Use automation for instant responses.

ü§ù Influencer & Partnership Outreach

‚ñº

üí° Pro Tip: Micro-influencers (5k-50k followers) often have better engagement rates than mega influencers. More authentic, budget-friendly.

üîê Cybersecurity & Data Privacy

‚ñº
  • Password Management Use LastPass, 1Password, or Bitwarden. Create 16+ character passwords with symbols. Never reuse passwords. Change every 90 days.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) Enable on ALL accounts: Google, Facebook, email, CRM, payment platforms. Use authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy).
  • Client Data Protection Never share login credentials publicly. Use encrypted password sharing (1Password shared vaults). Log out after each session.
  • GDPR Compliance Basics Get consent before collecting data. Allow users to access/delete their data. Have privacy policy. Report breaches within 72 hours.
  • Secure File Sharing Use Google Drive (password-protected links), Dropbox, or WeTransfer. Set expiration dates. Never email sensitive files unencrypted.
  • Phishing & Scam Prevention Verify sender email addresses. Don't click suspicious links. Check for HTTPS on websites. Report phishing attempts immediately.
  • Device Security Keep OS and apps updated. Use antivirus (Windows Defender, Malwarebytes). Enable firewall. Lock screen when away (5 min timeout).
  • Backup Strategy 3-2-1 Rule: 3 copies, 2 different media, 1 offsite. Use cloud backup (Google Drive, Backblaze). Weekly backup schedule minimum.
üí° Pro Tip: Never use public Wi-Fi for work without VPN. Use NordVPN or ExpressVPN to encrypt your connection and protect client data.

üöÄ Productivity & Workflow Optimization

‚ñº
  • Daily Task Template Morning (8-10AM): Check emails, respond to comments, post scheduled content. Midday (10-12PM): Content creation, design work. Afternoon (1-4PM): Strategy, analytics, meetings. Evening (4-6PM): Planning tomorrow, admin tasks.

  • File Organization System Structure: Client Name > Platform > Year > Month. Name files: YYYYMMDD_Description.png. Use cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox). Weekly cleanup.
  • Avoiding Burnout Tips Take 5-min breaks every hour. 30-min lunch break away from screen. Set work hours (no checking after 6PM). Weekend detox from social media. Exercise 3x weekly.
  • Time-Blocking Technique Dedicate 2-hour blocks for deep work. No interruptions during blocks. Use Focus mode on phone. Schedule breaks between blocks. Most productive: 9-11AM, 2-4PM.
  • Automation Tools to Use Zapier: Connect apps (email to Trello). IFTTT: Social media cross-posting. Buffer/Hootsuite: Schedule posts. TextExpander: Canned responses. Grammarly: Auto proofreading.
üí° Pro Tip: Batch similar tasks together. Create all graphics Monday, write all copy Tuesday. Context-switching kills productivity. Stay in one mode.

üåç Working with International Clients

‚ñº
  • Time Zone Management Know client's timezone (EST, PST, GMT). Use World Clock app. Schedule overlap hours (PH evening = US morning). Set calendar to both timezones.
  • Cultural Communication Tips Be direct but polite with Western clients. Avoid "po" and "opo". Use "I'll handle this" not "I'll try". Underpromise, overdeliver always.
  • Payment Methods PayPal (3.9% + ‚Ç±15 fee), Wise/TransferWise (lowest fees 0.5-1%), Payoneer (good for marketplaces), Direct bank transfer (rare, high fees).
  • Payment Terms & Invoicing Net-15 or Net-30 common. Invoice on 1st or 15th monthly. Use PayPal invoices or Wave/FreshBooks. Include VAT if registered (12%).
  • Tax Considerations Register as self-employed with BIR. File quarterly income tax (8% or graduated). Keep all payment receipts. Consult accountant for ‚Ç±250k+ annual income.
  • Professional Communication Use proper grammar (Grammarly helps). Respond within 24 hours max. Video calls: good lighting, quiet background. Professional email signature.
  • Building Trust Remotely Weekly check-ins with updates. Share screen during calls. Be transparent about challenges. Deliver before deadlines. Ask for testimonials.
  • Long-Term Client Retention Anticipate needs proactively. Suggest improvements. Stay updated on their industry. Send holiday greetings. Be reliable and consistent.
üí° Pro Tip: Set "working hours" in email signature (e.g., 9AM-6PM PHT / 8PM-5AM EST). Manage expectations. Use Calendly for easy scheduling.
